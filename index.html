Introducing Aabo Sales App

01

The Perfect All-in-one Dashboard

Instantly access your current earnings, latest rankings, average profit per deal, and even your personalized weekly/daily/monthly motivation and goals – right at your fingertips. You can also quickly get the exact data you need to determine the most profitable deal for you or even zero in on crucial areas needed to boost profitability.

02

Easy One-tap Account Creation

Creating an account has never been so easy and effortless. Thanks to Aabo’s powerful one-tap account creation process, you’ll be able to instantly auto-populate an address, quickly verify home ownership, and even run a credit analysis on the fly.

03

Quick Equipment Selection & Removal

Now you can get the exact equipment you need with a touch of a simple button. Simply choose the panel type you desire and the proper equipment that works with that particular panel will instantly appear. If you want to add or take away pieces of equipment, you can do so instantly with one touch.

04

Monthly Monitoring – The Smart Way

Thanks to Aabo’s simplicity and accuracy, you’ll be able to do monthly monitoring the smart way. This means no fuss and no added confusion. Auto calculates state and local taxes (if necessary). Get the exact information you need in a simple, fast and efficient way to help you move quickly to the next step.

05

Payment Collection & Billing Is a Breeze

Say goodbye to funding holds due to payment issues. Now payments and billing are a breeze thanks to Aabo. Easily scan a personal check or credit card and have all billing information automatically populated for you. Aabo will even send the tech a friendly reminder if the billing information needs to be collected after the installation.

06

Get Contracts Signed Fast Thanks To E-Sign

Get those lucrative contracts signed as quickly as possible thanks to Aabo’s exclusive E-Sign feature. All rep and tech signatures are now pre-signed and will automatically appear on all necessary forms. All customers have to do is sign the forms with their finger, tap “Accept and Sign”, and it’s all done!

07

Fully Automated Video Welcome Calls

Aabo has completely revolutionized and automated the entire welcome call process to under 2 minutes in order to save you critical time. This means no more dreaded phone calls or endless hold times. Aabo’s automated video welcome calls can be paused, resumed or even replayed if a customer needs further clarification.

08

Instant Scheduling & Tech Availability

Aabo offers instant visibility on tech availability directly from a Smartphone or tablet. Easily and instantly schedule a tech right from the app. Not only will Aabo automatically notify the chosen tech with the new work order, but it’ll also text the customer the ETA, name and picture of the newly assigned tech.

08

Easily Track Your Personal Earnings

Now you can track all payments from your work directly on Aabo – and down to specific time frames. You’ll be able to view average pay per job, average earnings (daily, weekly and monthly), full earnings (weekly, monthly, yearly) – and even review all non-fundables! Non-fundables are what you’d earn if all non-fundables were adjusted and fixed.
